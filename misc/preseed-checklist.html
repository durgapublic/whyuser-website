90-Day Execution Rhythm

A disciplined weekly cadence and clear monthly milestones are crucial for maintaining momentum.

Weekly Cadence

  • Mon: Pipeline review (stages, objections).
  • Tue: 2 interviews + outbound block (50 tailored emails).
  • Wed: Product reliability + QA rubric review.
  • Thu: Design-partner calls + case study progress.
  • Fri: Experiment log updates; KPI export; founder retro.

Milestones

  • Day 30: 10 interviews; 150 waitlist; 2 LOIs; Trust FAQ live.
  • Day 60: 30+ reports; forward-rate ≥25%; 4 LOIs; 2 paying logos.
  • Day 90: 50+ reports; forward-rate ≥30%; 5–10 LOIs/paying logos.
